My Key Trees

Resumy (Tree)

Resumy is an AI-powered resume builder and assistant that uses large-model APIs to analyze, generate, and optimize resumes, cover letters, and outreach/cold-mail content.

Live Preview

Assignme (tree)

AI-powered tool for generating handwritten responses from PDFs. This tool can complete any assignment in your handwriting style, providing personalized, handwritten documents effortlessly. Just assign your work and get it done in your own handwriting.

Live Preview

Aktu-resources (oldest tree)

AKTU Resources was a one-stop destination for AKTU students, offering free PDF Quantum books, past year papers, lab manuals, and study notes. Within just a few months, it gained a strong user base of 28,000 students across AKTU. However, due to unforeseen and unmanageable circumstances, I had to shut it down.
